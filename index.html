Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01/10/2024

Zippe Transport Ltd ("Zippe", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose personal information of our Drivers, Customers, and Business-to-Business (B2B) clients ("you" or "your") when you interact with our rideshare platform and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Customers

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture (if uploaded).
  • Location Data: Pickup and drop-off locations, real-time location data during ride usage, and route history.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, payment history, and billing address.
  • Communication: Messages between customers and drivers (e.g., in-app chat).
  • Usage Data: App interaction and analytics data (e.g., app usage patterns, IP address, device information).

1.2 Information from Drivers

  • Personal Details: Name, address, email, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Driver Verification: Government-issued NI number, UK driving license, DBS information, vehicle registration details, insurance information, MOT information, and vehicle hire agreement.
  • Location Data: Real-time location data, driving routes, and trip history.
  • Payment and Tax Information: Bank account details, tax identification numbers, payment history, and related financial information.
  • Vehicle Information: Vehicle type, condition, and other vehicle-related details.

1.3 Information from Business-to-Business Clients

  • Business Information: Company name, business address, tax identification numbers, and contact details of company representatives.
  • Payment Information: Business bank details and transaction history.
  • Communication: Any correspondence between Zippe and your business.

2. How We Use the Information

2.1 For Customers

  • To facilitate ride bookings and match customers with drivers.
  • To process payments and manage payment accounts.
  • To enhance the customer experience by providing real-time location updates, trip history, and recommendations.
  • To improve our app functionality and troubleshoot technical issues.
  • To ensure safety by monitoring ride activities and providing support in case of emergencies.
  • To communicate with you regarding rides, promotions, or policy changes.

2.2 For Drivers

  • To verify identity and qualifications for providing rideshare services.
  • To manage ride requests, routing, and navigation.
  • To process payments, tax, and earnings.
  • To provide support and resolve disputes.
  • To ensure driver and passenger safety by monitoring ride activity.

2.3 For Business Clients

  • To facilitate and manage service agreements, contracts, and rideshare services provided to employees or clients.
  • To process and manage invoices, payments, and financial records.
  • To communicate with businesses regarding service improvements, support, or updates.

3. Sharing of Information

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our platform (e.g., payment processors, background check providers, cloud storage providers, etc.). These providers are only permitted to use your information in line with our instructions and the law.

3.2 Law Enforcement and Regulatory Authorities

We may disclose your personal information when required by law or if necessary to protect the safety, rights, or property of Zippe, its users, or others. This includes sharing information with law enforcement agencies and local authorities for safety-related issues or fraud prevention.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred to the new business owners. In such a case, we will provide notice before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as is necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or to comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations. When no longer required, we will securely delete or anonymize your data.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Data Portability: You can request that we provide your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Objection: You can object to the processing of your personal data for marketing or other purposes.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at Privacy@zippe.co.uk.

6. Security of Your Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet or data storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.
